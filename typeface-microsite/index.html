Slice Serif?

Slice Serif is designed to get the sharpness and precision of a knife, balancing elegance with strength. It modernizes the traditional serif style with bold, clean cuts and confident forms.

Design Process

Starting from sketches of various knife blades, Slice Serif evolved through the study of light, edges, and dynamic shapes, translating their visual sharpness into letterforms.

Key Features

  • Blade-inspired sharp serifs
  • Strong contrast between thick and thin strokes
  • Modern proportions with a classic spirit
  • Elegant curves and sturdy stance

Applications

Perfect for magazine headlines, fashion branding, and editorial design. Slice Serif adds a bold and refined touch to any visual composition.
